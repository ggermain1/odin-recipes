Lasagna

Lasagna: A classic family diner originating from Italy

This is a traditional lasagna recipe

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • mozzorella cheese
  • parmasean cheese
  • minced onions
  • crushed garlic
  • tomato sauce
  • italian seasoning
  • ground beef

Instructions

  1. gather ingredients
  2. cook ground beef
  3. mix tomato sauce and seasonings
  4. add onions
  5. boil noodles
  6. mix cheeses in a bowl
  7. assemble lasagna
  8. bake for 25 minutes
